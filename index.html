What it is

An open source collection of functions, building on the basic features of JS, that enables writing of clear, concise and expressive code. Among the superpowers that sinful introduces, some are well-known:

  • Currying
  • Composition
  • Automatic, unobtrusive memoization
  • ...

And some are new findings that have proven to be beautiful:

  • Liberation
  • Enslavement
  • Flipped composition
  • ...

Check out the API section of the project wiki for a comprehensive listing and the relevant descriptions.

Requirements

A working ES5 environment.

How to contribute

We're always looking for ways to improve. Here are a few ways in which you can help:

  • Spread the word about sinful and get involved in our discussions
